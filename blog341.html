草菅人命的人，不配做人

2020年9月11日 · 吕合

草菅人命的人，不配做人，他却做了皇帝，或者统治者！
于是，被统治与奴役的人们、便向往着自己能像皇帝一样的占有别人的种种权力、甚至占有别人的生命！
于是就有了、崇拜那些草菅人命的、达官贵人的威权思想！
于是，我们看到这些个国家的人，很多都疯了一样的追求权力、拥护权力，以获得奴役、掠夺别人的生命的机会！虽然大多数都飞蛾投火，丢掉了自己的生命、做了他们向往的统治者桌上的俎上肉！但是，他们依旧像病毒一样的涌向世界！
看看崇尚威权的粉红们，哪怕是被扒的精光，也一样趴在地上，留下一条血印，向火堆爬至剩下最后一口肮脏的气息！
令人不解的是，号称已经进入到文明世界的——这个世界上，不仅诞生了这样的、庞大的、以掠夺别人生命为荣耀的统治集团！而且，还让他们做联合国的常任理事国，到处煽风点火，咄咄逼人，气势如虹的、伪装都不穿的，就这么赤裸裸的剥夺别人的权力、生命的耀武扬威！2020/9/10

Sylvia Zhang - Translation

A person who does not care about lives does not deserve to be a man, but he has become an emperor or a ruler!
Therefore, the ruled and enslaved yearn for themselves to possess power and lives of the people like their emperor!
It produces the authoritarian thought of high-ranking officials, worshiping those who do not care about people's lives!
As a result, we see that many people in these countries are crazy to pursue power and support power in order to obtain opportunities for enslavement and plundering others' lives! Although most of them are just like moths flying into fire, losing their lives and becoming the meat on the table of the ruler they yearned for, they still flock to the world like virus!
Look at the those who advocate authority, even if they are stripped off, they still lie on the ground, leaving a blood mark, and crawling towards the fire until the last dirty breath!
What is puzzling is that the world that claims to have entered civilization not only gave birth to such huge ruling groups who takes robbing others' lives as glory, moreover, let them become permanent members of the United Nations. The groups fan flames everywhere, aggressively imposing power without disguise, and nakedly depriving others of their power and life!
