脸书拟文，仅仅为了朋友；政治评析，仅仅为了后代

2020年9月20日 · 吕合

太忙了，虽然几个星期没写文章，但，我每天一定会抽时间看看朋友们都在干些什么、说些什么，以谢挂念！
人在关键的时候、特别需要的时候，都会寻求帮助！我希望我会帮到你们！
朋友们，祝你们快乐！

Sylvia Zhang - Translation

This Facebook is only for friends; the political commentary is only for future generations.
I am too busy. Although I haven't written an article for a few weeks, I will definitely take time every day to see what my friends are doing and talking about.
People will ask for help at critical times and when they are in special need! I hope I can help!
My friends, wish you happiness!
